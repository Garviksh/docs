## 🧭 Welcome to Your Command Center

The **Dashboard** is your launchpad for managing every stage of your contract lifecycle. It’s intelligently designed to provide:

- 🔍 Quick navigation
- 🧠 AI-driven insights
- 🔐 Smart contract access
- 📊 Real-time activity tracking

---

### 🔎 Smart Navigation Tools

#### **Search Icon**
Easily locate contracts by Contract ID or Name using the **Search Bar** – built for speed and precision.

#### **Profile Icon**
Quickly access:

- 🏠 `Home`: Navigate to your dashboard
- 👤 `Profile`: Update your personal settings or password
- 🏢 `Company Settings`: Customize your organization-level preferences
- 📘 `Tutorial`: Access in-platform help
- 🚪 `Logout`: Securely sign out

#### **Notification Icon**
Stay ahead of your tasks with **real-time alerts** for approvals, updates, and workflows – right at your fingertips.
